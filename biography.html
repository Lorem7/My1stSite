Oleg Vladimirovich
Pozidaev

Oleg was born in the city of Vladivostok, in the far east of Russia in 1973. When he was yet five years of age his mother noticed that her son showed abilities for drawing, and it was at that time that Oleg stated that he would be an artist. At the age of ten he enrolled in a local art school for kids, where his natural talent blossomed under the guidance of the skilled artist Galina Stepanovna Tsapko.

At the age of 14 he entered an Art Collage, painting and art teachers department in Vladivostok where he received professional skills and a degree. The artist was especially impressed by the love for landscape and still life paintings. Trying to utilize any spare moment with his easel he would leave the city to go to the countryside art retreats to do nature study drawings.
